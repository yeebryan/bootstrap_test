ABOUT US

History Achievements Technology Our People Our Group Members

Founded In 1980, Unicurd began producing hygienically packed tofu in a factory in Aljunied. Unicurd introduces to Singapore the first hygienically packed pasteurized silken tofu, in hygienically sealed boxes and in tubes which was an instant success.

By 1982 the business expanded and soon run out of operation space. A bigger factory in Ang Mo kio was bought over to meet the increasing business demands.

In 1996 the company moved into its newly constructed 2700 square meter factory in Senoko, with over S$3 million of state-of-the-art tofu manufacturing equipment.

In April 2008, Vitasoy International Holdings acquired the entire issued share capital of Unicurd. As a result, Unicurd becomes a wholly owned subsidiary of the Vitasoy Group.

In 2008, Unicurd was acquired by Vitasoy International Holdings Limited (VIHL) Limited through its subsidiary Vitasoy Investment (Singapore) Pte. Limited. With this, Unicurd can expand its soya product business in an international scale and provide a strategic role for the group consolidation in the Soy.
